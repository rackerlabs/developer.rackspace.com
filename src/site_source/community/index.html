Community & Outreach

Rackspace is committed to supporting communities. We offer small grants for workshops, hackdays, meetups, and conferences. We can also send Rackers to speak at your event and/or teach and mentor your group. Let us know what kind of support you need, and a Racker will reach out to you.

What type of events does Rackspace sponsor?

We sponsor large and small conferences, meetups, workshops, hackathons, and other kinds of tech community gatherings.

What kind of sponsorships do you provide?

We usually offer financial support, but we can also make you stickers and t-shirts, send you pizza and snacks, offer you event space. Let us know what you're looking for.

Can I get a Racker to speak or teach at my event?

Yes! We love to attend and participate in the events we sponsor. If a Racker is available and in the area, we'll send them to your event.
